# vitiligo.ton — Manifesto

> Every person with vitiligo is a star. Together, we are a constellation that cannot be faked.

## What this is

vitiligo.ton is the first counter of people with vitiligo in the world — built on the TON blockchain so that no one can fake or inflate the numbers. It is not a lottery, not a marketplace, not a "Web3 collectible app." It is a collective monument.

Every person who joins connects their wallet, sends a symbolic 1 TON, and becomes a real, permanent star on a shared cosmic canvas, visible to the whole world. It is an act of recognition: *I exist. I am not alone.*

## The constellation is you

When you upload your photo, an algorithm finds the vitiligo patches on your skin and turns them into a personal constellation — unique to you, built from the actual shape of your own body. It is not decoration. It is a literal map of you, turned into stars.

You can also add your own story — "appeared on my hand when I was 12" — and it becomes part of your NFT forever. This is not an investment. It is a memory and a sense of belonging.

## Rarity is fate, not a purchase

Every mint costs exactly the same: 1 TON. There is no "pay more for a better star." About 1 in 10 mints reveals a rare golden constellation — and every 100th person in the whole project is guaranteed one, no matter what. Rarity here is luck, not status you can buy. That mirrors vitiligo itself: something that happens to you, not something you choose.

## Why blockchain, specifically

Every registration is a real transaction from a real wallet. The count cannot be quietly padded with bots or fake accounts behind the scenes — anyone can verify it. That honesty is the foundation the whole project stands on.

## In one sentence

**Every person with vitiligo is a star, and together we are a constellation that cannot be faked.**
